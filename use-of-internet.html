The Purpose of Internet ranges

Voice Communications over the internet is beneficial in many ways.


Here’s how:
  1. Cost Effectiveness
    Long distance calls can be made over the Internet with no extra cost. To use a telephone to make international calls will only add to your bill. But making calls over the internet wont.
  2. Flexibilty And Scalability
    The VOIP is adaptable and you can “Bring your own Carrier” this means that by using the Cloud Phone System you can reduce the amount of equipment in the office. For example; an office can have multiple telephones all connecting over the one line whereas the CPS through VOIP Provider connects to the public switched telephone network allowing you to make calls from the computer replacing the need for telephones and reducing charges. The computer can act as a 2in1.
  3. Feature Rich Functionality
    Has features that can call-forward over the Internet network just as a phone would, voicemail-to-email which is like leaving a voicemail but in email form.
  4. Portability and Accesibility
    No telephone line needed just Internet
  5. Integration Capabilities
    VOIP systems can access other business tools and applications that stream workflow and task, keeping it all together so that one application won’t over power another.
  6. HD-Voice Quality
    Makes the sound and connection clear improving the line with no interference or coverage loss. Maintain a clear and strong line.
  7. Environmental Benefits
    Reduce the amount of hardware devices lowering energy consumption.

File Downloading

The purpose of file downloading is to download files or data from the computer over a large network server. Even if you do not select something to be downloaded the computer is already downloading data with ever click you make on a browser. Without downloading the internet would not be in use. The amount you download has a bandwidth and the bandwidth your service provider has is the amount you can receive. The larger the bandwidth the more data is available to you to view. Downloading means you have a permanent copy of that file. To stream a file is only giving you access while in use.

  1. HTTP
    The most common one people use, it downloads file data as you browse.
  2. FTP
    Is an encrypted protocol with passwords. This is how files or documents with passwords can be sent and downloaded for the user.
  3. P2P
    Downloads are sent and received by the user without a host. The host is usually the browser in this case it’s the one member sending a file/document to another over an application software.
